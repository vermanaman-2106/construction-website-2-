Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical residential renovation takes 2-4 months, while new construction can take 6-12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations to discuss your project, understand your needs, and provide preliminary estimates. This helps you make informed decisions about your project.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured, including general liability and workers' compensation insurance. We can provide copies of our licenses and insurance certificates upon request.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding regions. For projects outside our primary service area, we may charge additional travel fees. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.

Do you offer financing options?

We work with several financing partners to help make your project affordable. We can provide information about various financing options during your consultation.

Do you follow NBC (National Building Code) standards?

Absolutely! All our projects are designed and constructed in strict compliance with the National Building Code (NBC) and local building regulations. We ensure every project meets or exceeds these standards for safety, quality, and legal compliance.

How do you handle building permits and approvals?

We handle all building permits and regulatory approvals as part of our comprehensive service. Our team is experienced in navigating the permit process, ensuring all documentation meets NBC requirements, and coordinating with local authorities to get your project approved efficiently.

How do you provide building quotes and estimates?

We provide detailed, transparent building quotes based on your specific requirements. Our estimates include material costs, labor, permits, and all associated fees. We offer both preliminary estimates for budgeting and detailed quotes after site visits. All quotes are valid for 30 days and include a breakdown of costs by category.
